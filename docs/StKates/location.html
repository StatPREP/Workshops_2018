Location information

Fontbonne Hall, rooms 212 and 214. The rooms are accessible by entering the building from the North door (facing Randolph).

Both days’ meetings are in Fontbonne Hall on the Randolph Avenue campus in Saint Paul. (Building number 5 on the map.)

If possible, park in the L-shaped guest parking (shown in orange on the map). If that’s full, park in the O’Shaughnessy Lot. (Continue past the guest parking lot until the T intersection. Turn left, continuing 500 feet to the big parking lot.)

Participants will be sent a parking permit to be printed out and which should be displayed at St. Kates. There will also be printed copies available at the workshop.
