Getting to College of the Canyons

Driving:

Get directions via this Google map to University Center (UCEN)

Flying in:

You can fly into LAX or BUR, Burbank airport. Burbank is closer, but LAX will have more flight options. If youre flying into LAX, please note that traffic is bad all the time, but particularly bad from 3pm to 7pm if youre flying in at those times. Either airport you will need either a rental car or Uber/Lyft to get to College of the Canyons.

Lodging

I recommend the Hyatt, its the closest to the campus and across the street from our mall where there are restaurants and shops. Here are a few options though for you to check into.
